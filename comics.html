Keybinds for your convenience:

  • [+]: Zoom In
  • [-]: Zoom Out
  • [<-]: Previous
  • [->]: Next
  • [R]: Random
  • [F]: First
  • [Shift]+[<-]: First
  • [L]: Current
  • [Shift]+[->]: Current
